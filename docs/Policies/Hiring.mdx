# Hiring

We hire as contractor and employee of record (employee). 
The below sections focus on different hiring options.

## Hiring in Germany

### Full-time Employee

1. **Download** the "Hiring in Germany - FT Employee - Template" in the [HR workspace](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/149CKkOlldop5jSEL1ux83F8Gpj1nNC9l?usp=sharing).
2. **Extract** the zip file, **open** the folder and **rename** the content to the name of the new employee.
3. **Upload** the folder to the HR workspace.
4. **Fill out** the "arbeitsvertrag-vollzeit".
5. **Send** the "arbeitsvertrag-vollzeit" and the "personalbogen.pdf" to the new employee for review.
    > Dear [Name],
    >
    > We prepared your employment contract: [docs link to arbeitsvertrag-vollzeit with **comment access**]. 
    > Please review it and **comment** in the document if you have any questions or concerns. 
    > Please leave out the signature and date section. We will later use DocuSign for the signature process.
    >
    > Further, please download the "Personalbogen.pdf" and fill it out. 
    > Again, please leave out the signature and date section. 
    >
    > Once you have both completed. Please ping us again.
    >
    > We are looking forward to working with you.
    >
    > Best wishes,
    >
    > [Your Name]
6. **Add Signatures.** If both is completed, upload documents as pdf on DocuSign and
send it for signature to yourself & the new employee
(Request from the COO the DocuSign license if you don't have one)
7. **Send it tax consultant/ register.** Once signed, send both files to the
tax consultant for social benefit registration of the employee.
    > Dear [Name],
    >
    > We just hired [Name employee] as a full-time employee.
    > Attached you will find the "arbeitsvertrag" and the "personalbogen".
    >
    > Please let me know if you need anything else.
    >
    > Best wishes,
    >
    > [Your Name]
