## Fonts
**Headlines:** Oswald<br>
**Any other text:** IBM plex sans
(alternatively: Roboto)<br>
Both fonts are sans-serif typefaces and exist in Google Docs.<br><br>
In  documents, it is suggested to use a size 42 Oswald (black) for titles, size 26 Oswald (red) for heading 1, and size 11 IBM plez sans (black) for text.

## Colors
Red: #e31937, RGB: 277, 25, 55 <br>
Sage: #7c9885, RGB: 124, 152, 133<br>
White: #ffffff, RGB: 255,255,255 <br>
Grey: #efefef, RGB: 239, 239, 239 <br>
