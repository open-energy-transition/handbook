# About OET

Open Energy Transition (OET) is a nonprofit organization. We want to accelerate the global energy transition with open-source software and open data. Our tools are transparent, flexible, and customizable to your needs. We help clients with everything from software and training to targeted studies.


# The Elevator Pitch (short)
<i>Imagine you are taking the elevator with someone you don't know - but who might just be a potential partner or customer. They ask: "So what does your company do?" and you can answer in 30 seconds:</i>

We provide open-source modelling tools for practical energy system planning. By advancing open-source models and open data, we empower transmission system operators, energy regulators, and NGOs to work together toward a secure, transparent, and data-driven energy future. Our mission is to accelerate the energy transition and make open modelling a mainstream part of energy planning practice by 2030. This is not a distant vision — the ecosystem is ready, and impactful collaborations are already underway.
