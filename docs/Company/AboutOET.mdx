# About OET

<ControlledDocBanner />

Open Energy Transition (OET) is a non-profit organization and software company. We want to accelerate the global transition to sustainable energy by making open source energy modeling. Our tools are transparent, flexible and can be customized after your needs and we help with software, studies, and capacity building. 


# The Elevator Pitch (short)
<i>Imagine now that you are taking the elevator with someone you don't know but who might just be a potential partner or customer. They ask: "So what does your company do", and you can answer in 30 seconds:</i>

Open Energy Transition (OET) is a non-profit organization founded in 2023. We have an energy planning toolbox that is cost-efficient, flexible, and transparent for modern, open source energy planning locally and globally.

We want to accelerate the global transition to sustainable and reliable energy with high resolution energy planning tools. We offer consultancy studies, software development, and technical and organizational support for PyPSA.



# The longer one
<i>A slightly longer one? Here you go. This is if you are taking the elevator from ground floor to 10th. Or just taking a really old one.</i>

Open Energy Transition (OET) is a non-profit organization founded in 2023. Our open modeling toolbox enables cost-efficient, flexible, and transparent operational planning of modern energy infrastructure on a local and global scale.

Our mission is to accelerate the global transition to sustainable and reliable energy by providing high resolution open source energy planning tools and open data solutions. We make software development on PyPSA modeling, consultancy studies, capacity building with technical and organizational assistance. 

PyPSA tools are downloaded more than 230,000 times and are used in research and industry-related projects.
